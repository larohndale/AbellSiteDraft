About Team Abell

Founded in 2000 by Vincent Abell, a carpenter by trade with over three decades of experience in commercial and residential construction. Vincent Abell Contracting has excelled in the last 16 years through a strict commitment to outstanding design, craftsmanship, and customer service. Vincent Abell continues to serve as senior superintendent on a daily basis. To assist with each project, Vincent has a talented production team including supervisors, specialty contractors and skilled craftsman, e.g., plumbers, electricians, tile installers, trim carpenters, drywall finishers, painters, and more.
