About Team Abell

Vincent Abell Contracting is a family owned and operated full service General Contracting build firm. We specialize in custom remodeling & renovations in Louisville as well as surround counties. Services include home additions, historic preservation, kitchens, bathrooms, basements, decks, porches, dormer additions, screen rooms, load bearing wall removal, room additions, home improvements, and more.

Louisville Remodeling Experts

Since 2000, Vincent Abell Contracting's professional and experienced team members have provided expert remodeling services to many of Louisville's most established neighborhoods including the Highlands, St. Matthews, Audubon Park, Anchorage, Prospect, Glenview, Indian Hills, Old Louisville, Crestwood, Pewee Valley, Crescent Hill, and more.

Abell Approach

  1. Initial Consultation: Complimentary site visit by Brad Abell. We will discuss what you want to do, when you want to do it, and project budget.
  2. Design: Every great project starts with a vision. It is our job to help shape your vision in to reality. The design agreement is the first step in putting the plan in to action. We offer professional design-build services catered to your personal wish list and budget.
  3. Site Visit Walk Through: After detailed construction drawings are created, our team members and required specialty contractors will perform a site visit.
  4. Construction agreement: A detailed scope of work and bid package is put together for the project based on the completed drawings and agreed upon budget.
  5. Construction: We pull the permit and start construciton.
