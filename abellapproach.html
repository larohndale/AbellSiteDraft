Abell Approach

  1. 1. Initial Consultation: Complimentary site visit by Brad Abell. We will discuss what you want to do, when you want to do it, and project budget.
  2. 2. Design: Every great project starts with a vision. It is our job to help shape your vision in to reality. The design agreement is the first step in putting the plan in to action. We offer professional design-build services catered to your personal wish list and budget.
  3. 3. Site Visit Walk Through: After detailed construction drawings are created, our team members and required specialty contractors will perform a site visit.
  4. 4.Construction agreement: A detailed scope of work and bid package is put together for the project based on the completed drawings and agreed upon budget.
  5. 5. Construction: We pull the permit and start construciton.
